Data Deletion Request Instructions

This page explains how to request the deletion of your personal data associated with your account in Yocable, regardless of the login method you use (Facebook, Google, or Email/Password).

1. Data Managed by Login Providers (Facebook, Google)

You can revoke our application's access to your account via the settings of these providers. This action will remove the information that the provider shares with us.

1.1 For Facebook Logins:

  1. Go to your Facebook account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click on "Settings".
  3. In the left-hand menu, select "Apps and Websites".
  4. Find our application in the list, check the corresponding box, then click the "Remove" button.

1.2 For Google Logins:

  1. Go to your Google Account.
  2. In the left navigation panel, click on "Security".
  3. In the "Third-party apps with account access" panel, click on "Manage third-party access".
  4. Find our application in the list, click on it, then click "Remove Access".

2. Data Stored Directly by Yocable

Yocable stores data related to your learning progress, custom vocabulary lists, and preferences to enhance your experience. To request the complete deletion of this data, please email us at:

yocableapp@gmail.com

Please include the following details in your request:

  • Your username (if applicable)
  • The email address associated with your account
  • A clear statement requesting deletion of your data

We will confirm receipt and process your request within 30 days, following data protection regulations.
