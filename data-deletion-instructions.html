Instructions for Deleting Your Personal Data

This page explains how to request the deletion of your personal data associated with your account in our application, regardless of the login method you use (Facebook, Google, or Email/Password).

1. Data Managed by Login Providers (Facebook, Google)

You can revoke our application's access to your account via the settings of these providers. This action will remove the information that the provider shares with us.

1.1 For Facebook Logins:

  1. Go to your Facebook account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click on "Settings".
  3. In the left-hand menu, select "Apps and Websites".
  4. Find our application in the list, check the corresponding box, then click the "Remove" button.

1.2 For Google Logins:

  1. Go to your Google Account.
  2. In the left navigation panel, click on "Security".
  3. In the "Third-party apps with account access" panel, click on "Manage third-party access".
  4. Find our application in the list, click on it, then click "Remove Access".

2. Data Stored Directly by Our Application

In addition to the information managed by Facebook or Google, our application directly stores data related to your usage to enhance your learning experience. This includes your learning progress, custom vocabulary lists, words you've added or learned, and any application-specific preferences.

To request the complete deletion of all this data from our servers, regardless of your login method (Facebook, Google, or Email/Password), please send us an email at the following address:

yocableapp@gmail.com

In your email, please include the following information to help us identify your account and process your request quickly:

  • Your username in our application (if you have one).
  • The email address associated with the account you use to log in (even if it's a Facebook or Google account).
  • A clear request for the deletion of all your personal data related to the application.

We will acknowledge receipt of your request and process it within 30 days, in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

For more information, please consult our Privacy Policy.
