Data Deletion Request Instructions

This page explains how to request the deletion of your personal data associated with your account in Yocable, regardless of the login method you use (Google or Email/Password).

1. Data Managed by Google Login

You can revoke our application's access to your Google account through your Google Account settings. This removes the link between your account and Yocable.

  1. Go to your Google Account.
  2. In the left navigation panel, click on "Security".
  3. In the "Third-party apps with account access" panel, click on "Manage third-party access".
  4. Find Yocable in the list, click on it, then click "Remove Access".

2. Data Stored Directly by Yocable

Yocable stores certain information to improve your learning experience, such as your progress, vocabulary lists, and preferences. To permanently delete all this data, please send an email to:

yocableapp@gmail.com

Please include the following details in your request:

  • Your username (if applicable)
  • The email address associated with your account
  • A clear statement requesting the deletion of all your data

We will confirm receipt and process your request within 30 days, in accordance with data protection regulations.
