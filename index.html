Learn by swiping

Right if you know it, left if you don’t. That’s it. No menus mid-flow, no friction. Each gesture reinforces recall and keeps sessions short enough to fit anywhere in your day.

Why it works
  • Fast loops: dozens of cards in minutes.
  • Simple decisions: less cognitive clutter, more memory power.
  • Built-in audio: listen, repeat, and lock in pronunciation.

A smart algorithm to lock in words

Our spaced-repetition system (SRS) adapts to you. Words climb through 22 mastery tiers. A correct swipe spreads out the next review; a mistake pulls the card forward before it fades.

What’s under the hood
  • Adaptive intervals: longer for strong words, shorter for weak ones.
  • New word loop: tier 0 cards repeat until your first correct recall sticks.
  • Reverse practice: translate both ways to build active recall.
