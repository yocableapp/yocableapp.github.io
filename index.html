🧠 Smart Learning Made Simple

Yocable is an innovative mobile app that makes learning new vocabulary simple, fast, and fun. Using an intuitive swipe-based interface, you review words in seconds — swipe right if you know it, left if you don’t.

No pressure, no clutter — just pure learning flow. Every gesture strengthens your memory naturally.

🚀 The Intelligence Behind It

At the heart of Yocable lies a Spaced Repetition System (SRS) — a proven algorithm that adapts each session to your progress. Words you know appear less frequently, while tricky ones come back just when you’re about to forget them.

The result: faster recall, stronger memory, and effortless consistency. You don’t study harder — you study smarter.

🎮 Motivation That Lasts

Learning should be exciting — not a chore. With XP points, streaks, achievements, and progress charts, Yocable keeps you engaged, challenged, and rewarded.

Every session feels like a small win — and over time, those wins become fluency. 🏆

✍️ Personalized Learning

Customize your experience: add your own words, explore rich vocabulary themes (travel, business, culture, emotions...) and tailor the difficulty to your goals.

Yocable adapts to you — not the other way around.

🎨 Light, Beautiful, and Human

A calm and elegant interface that reflects your mood. Light and dark themes, clean typography, and soft gradients create a space that feels effortless.

Yocable isn’t just an app — it’s your daily pocket companion for growth. 🌤️

🌍 Speak the World’s Language

Every word you learn is a bridge to a new culture, a new friendship, a new opportunity. With Yocable, you’re not memorizing — you’re connecting.
