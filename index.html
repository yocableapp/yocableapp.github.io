Learn by Swiping

Right if you know it, left if you don’t — no menus in the way. Each gesture strengthens recall and keeps practice short enough to fit anywhere in your day.

Why it clicks
  • Fast loops: dozens of cards in minutes.
  • Micro-decisions: less clutter, more memory.
  • Built-in audio: hear, repeat, retain.

A Smart Algorithm That Adapts

Words climb through 22 mastery tiers. Correct answers push reviews further out; mistakes bring them forward before they fade — spaced repetition done right.

Under the hood
  • Adaptive intervals per word difficulty.
  • New-word loop: tier 0 repeats until first success sticks.
  • Reverse practice: translate both ways for active recall.
