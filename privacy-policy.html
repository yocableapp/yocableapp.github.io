Privacy Policy for Yocable

Last updated: June 24, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Yocable. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Data We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account via email, Google, or Facebook, we store your user ID, email address, and display name.
  • Progress Data: We record the words you learn, your progress (levels), streaks, and other statistics to personalize your learning experience.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the application, such as the themes you explore or the features you use.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the application's services.
  • To personalize your learning experience.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account or our services.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our application.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is stored securely and used solely for the operation of the Yocable application.

Deletion of Your Data

You can request the deletion of all your personal data at any time. Please refer to our data deletion instructions page for the detailed procedure.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at yocableapp@gmail.com.
